Exhibit A — Founding Principles

Practical, effective, and client-centred.

VNM Attorneys Inc was founded on an unwavering commitment to justice. We serve individuals, families, organisations and businesses across South Africa, and every matter we take on is built on five founding principles.

Article I

Integrity

We advise plainly and act consistently, whether or not anyone is watching.

Article II

Ethics

Our conduct follows the rules of the profession, not the convenience of the moment.

Article III

Accountability

We answer for our advice and keep clients informed at every stage of a matter.

Article IV

Professionalism

Diligence, discretion and respect define how we work with every client.

Article V

Commitment to Justice

We pursue outcomes that are fair, defensible, and in our clients' true interest.
